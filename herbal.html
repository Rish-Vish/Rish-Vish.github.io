Herbal Tea

Background image: by fcafotodigital at GettyImages at Tree Hugger

Herbal teas, also known as tisanes, are infusions made from herbs, spices, or other plant materials. They are naturally caffeine-free and offer a wide range of flavors and health benefits.

Popular Herbs

  • Chamomile: Known for its calming and relaxing properties.
  • Peppermint: Refreshing and soothing for digestion.
  • Ginger: Spicy and warming, often used to relieve nausea.
  • Rooibos: A South African red tea with a sweet, nutty flavor.

Preparation

Herbal teas are generally brewed with boiling water (212°F or 100°C) for 5-10 minutes, depending on the type of herb. They can be enjoyed hot or cold.
